# Clipmart — Pain Points & Business Plan

## Pain Points
- **Law Firms:** Lead generation is expensive and low-quality. They spend $100+ per lead only for most to be unqualified.
- **Claimants:** Individual claims are small ($500), making them hard to monetize alone.
- **Solution:** Clipmart aggregates 10,000+ identical $500 claims into a single "Claim Bundle" that a law firm can buy for $50k+ instantly.

## Revenue: 10% Transaction Fee
- Marketplace takes 10% of every claim bundle sale.
- Example: $100k claim bundle sale = $10,000 revenue for the platform.

## PRD
- **✅ Built:** B2B Marketplace Dashboard, Escrow Split API.
- **⬜ Planned:** Smart Contract for claim ownership transfer, Automated KYC for law firms, Claim quality scoring system.

## Roadmap
- **Phase 1 ✅:** Infrastructure.
- **Phase 2:** Integration with the JaaS Fleet (SueBot, FlightComp, etc.) to funnel claims.
- **Phase 3:** High-volume B2B auction engine.
